Paphos Highlights & Traditional Cuisine

Discover Paphos’ History & Experience Local Traditions

Duration: 8 hr   |   From: €320

Pick-up: From your hotel

Tour Description

Explore the birthplace of Aphrodite, the UNESCO-listed archaeological park of Paphos, and discover the rich myths and history of Cyprus. Visit ancient ruins, enjoy local traditions, and savor authentic Cypriot dishes, making for an unforgettable experience.

Excursion Highlights

  1. Pickup from Hotel: Start your journey with a comfortable ride from your hotel.
  2. Aphrodite’s Birthplace (30 min): Visit the legendary birthplace of the Goddess of Love, steeped in mythology.
  3. Archaeological Park of Paphos (60 min): UNESCO-listed site with stunning mosaics of gods, heroes, and mythical creatures.
  4. Paphos Harbour (45 min): Free time to take photos, stroll, and buy souvenirs.
  5. Basilica & Saint Paul’s Pillar (30 min): Explore the 5th-century Basilica and the Apostle Paul’s Column.
  6. Tombs of the Kings (60 min): Ancient necropolis, including Cyprus’ largest tomb.
  7. Sophia’s House, Ledimbou (2 hrs): Bake bread, make halloumi, and enjoy a meze lunch in a traditional home.
  8. Coffee Break (20 min): Learn to make Cyprus coffee using a ‘mbriki’ before return.
  9. Drop off at Hotel: End the day with unforgettable memories.
