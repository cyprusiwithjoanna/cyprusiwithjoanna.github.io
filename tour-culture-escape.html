Cyprus Culture Escape: History & Wine

From Kolossi Castle to Troodos vineyards – discover the rich culture and flavors of Cyprus.

Duration: 8 hr   |   From: €300

Pick-up: From hotels in Protaras, Ayia Napa, Larnaca, Limassol, or Paphos

Tour description

A full-day journey through Cyprus’s layered history, traditional villages, and family-owned vineyards. Enjoy authentic food, hands-on dessert making, and a relaxed tasting at a local winery.

Excursion Highlights

  1. Kolossi Castle (45 minutes): Begin your day with a visit to the medieval Kolossi Castle near Limassol. This historic fortress dates back to the Middle Ages and offers a glimpse into Cyprus's storied past. Explore its well-preserved rooms and enjoy panoramic views from the top.
  2. Ancient City of Kourion (45 minutes): Next, visit Kourion, an ancient Greco-Roman city perched on a hill overlooking the coast. Marvel at the iconic amphitheater, dating back to the 2nd century BC, and explore the House of Eustolios, once a private villa and later a public recreation center during the Early Christian period.
  3. Pissouri Village – Dessert Making and Lunch (1.5 hours): Travel to the charming village of Pissouri, known for its picturesque vineyards and stunning Mediterranean views. Here, you’ll participate in a hands-on experience making Pourekia, a traditional Cypriot dessert. Enjoy your homemade treat after a delicious meal at a local family-run tavern.
  4. Omodos Village (1 hour): Journey to the Troodos Mountains and visit Omodos, a quaint village with rich history and traditional architecture. Highlights include the Monastery of the Holy Cross (12th century) and the restored 16th-century Linos wine press. Afterward, enjoy free time to explore the cobbled streets, shop for souvenirs, or simply soak in the village ambiance.
  5. Winery Visit and Wine Tasting (45 minutes): Conclude your village visit with a stop at a family-owned winery outside Omodos. Sample a variety of traditional Cypriot wines and spirits while learning about the island’s winemaking traditions.
  6. Coffee Break and Return to Hotel (20 minutes): Relax with a Cyprus coffee on the way back, reflecting on a day filled with history, culture, and unforgettable experiences.
