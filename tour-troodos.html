Troodos Mountain Tour: Nature & Heritage

Discover the timeless beauty of Cyprus' mountains

Duration: 8 hr   |   From: €300

Pick-up: From your hotel

Tour Description

Experience the stunning beauty of the Troodos Mountains with this unforgettable excursion. This tour offers a perfect blend of scenic landscapes, historical treasures, and authentic Cypriot flavors.

Excursion Options

Option 1: Hiking Adventure

  1. Pickup from the Hotel: Comfortable ride from Protaras, Ayia Napa, Larnaca, Limassol, or Paphos.
  2. Artemis Nature Trail Hike (2.5 hours): A 7-km circular trail on Mount Olympus with breathtaking views, lush flora, and mythological charm.
  3. Saint Nicholas tis Stegis Church (45 minutes): 11th-century UNESCO World Heritage church, renowned for its exquisite wall paintings.
  4. Lunch in a Local Tavern (1.5 hours): Enjoy a traditional meze feast with a drink.
  5. Kakopetria Village (1 hour): Wander the charming old streets with stone houses and scenic views.
  6. Coffee Break (20 minutes): Reflect on your day with a Cyprus coffee before returning.

Option 2: Cultural Exploration

  1. Pickup from the Hotel
  2. Lania Village (1 hour): Picturesque village with art galleries, quaint streets, and serene atmosphere.
  3. Galata Churches (1 hour): Explore two UNESCO churches adorned with stunning medieval frescoes.
  4. Saint Nicholas tis Stegis Church (45 minutes): Delve deeper into this 11th-century masterpiece.
  5. Lunch in a Local Tavern (1.5 hours): Traditional meze, savoring the diverse flavors of Cyprus.
  6. Kakopetria Village (1 hour): Discover cobbled streets and natural beauty.
  7. Coffee Break (20 minutes): Relax before heading back.

Tour Details

Price: €300 for one person; €30 for each additional person (max 6 people)
For 5–6 people: extra €60 for larger car rental.

Duration: Full day

Includes:

  • Transport in a luxury car
  • Licensed tourist guide
  • Traditional lunch with one drink

Languages: Dutch, English, Greek (translator available on request).
